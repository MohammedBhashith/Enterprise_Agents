HR Policy Document

Notice Period Policy:
Employees must serve a notice period of 60 days before leaving the company.

Casual Leave Policy:
Employees are allowed 12 casual leaves per year.

Sick Leave Policy:
Employees are allowed 10 sick leaves per year. Sick leave can be used for illness, medical appointments, or health emergencies.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees may request work from home with manager approval. Work from home is allowed based on project requirements and manager discretion.

Maternity Leave Policy:
Eligible female employees are entitled to maternity leave as per company policy and applicable labor laws.

Leave Approval Policy:
All leave requests must be submitted through the internal system. The manager must approve or reject the request.

Holiday Policy:
Leaves cannot be applied on official company holidays.
